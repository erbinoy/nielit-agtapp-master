 2.4 Operating System Simple Setting
 2.4.5 Adding and Removing Printers
Steps to Add a Printer :

1. Open Control panel.

2. Double click on Devices and Printers.

3. Click on Properties.

4. Follow the instructions given on the screen.

Steps to Remove a Printer :

1. Open Control panel.

2. Double click on Devices and Printers.

3. Click on Remove.

4. Follow the instructions given on the screen.
