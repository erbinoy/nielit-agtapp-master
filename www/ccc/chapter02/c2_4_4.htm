 2.4 Operating System Simple Setting
 2.4.4 Changing Mouse Properties
Steps for changing Mouse Properties:

1. Open Control panel.

2. Double click on Mouse option.

3. A mouse Properties dialog box will appear. Select the setting options from the panel and adjust the settings as you desire.

4. Click Apply.

5. Click OK.
