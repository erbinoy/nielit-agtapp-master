 2.4 Operating System simple setting
 2.4.2 Changing Display Properties

Windows 10 offers variety of options that enable you to change the way the graphical user interface or desktop behaves.

Steps to change the background screen :

1. Right click on Desktop and click Personalize.

2. Click on Background.

3. Select the desire background screen solid color or images present on your system.
And choose the appearance to fit the background.

Steps to change the Screen saver:

1. Right click on Desktop and click Personalize.

2. Click on Lock Screen.

3. Then click on Screen Saver Setting.

4. Select the desire Screen saver and set Screen saver timeout.

5. Click Apply.

6. Click OK.

Steps to change the colors setting:

1. Right click on Desktop and click Personalize.

2. Click on Colors.

3. Set the color settings as you desire.

Steps to change the theme and other related settings:

1. Right click on Desktop and click Personalize.

2. Click on Themes.

3. Select Themes setting to change the settings of theme.

4. For other related settings choose the options such as Advanced sound settings, Desktop icon settings, Mouse pointer settings that are present on Related Settings.

Steps to change the Start menu setting:

1. Right click on Desktop and click Personalize.

2. Click on Start.

3. Set the settings.
