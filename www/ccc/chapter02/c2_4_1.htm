 2. INTRODUCTION TO GUI BASED OPERATING SYSTEM
 2.4 Operating system simple setting
 2.4.1 Changing System Date and Time
Steps for changing Date and Time:

1. Open Control panel.

2. Double click on Date and Time.

3. Click on Change Date and Time.

4. Set the Date and Time as you desire.

5. Then click OK.
