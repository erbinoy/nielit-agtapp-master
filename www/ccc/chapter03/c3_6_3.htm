 3.6 Table Manipulation
3.6.3 Alignment of Text in cell

You can change the alignment of the contents in a cell by performing the following steps :

1. Select the Content or click the cell or table that you want to align.

2. Click Layout present at the Tool bar.

3. In the Alignment tab, choose the alignment type as you desired among the nine types of alignment.
