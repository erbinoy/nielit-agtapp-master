 3.6 Table Manipulation
3.6.4 Delete / Insertion of row or column

Deletion of a row or column :

1. Select the cell of the row or column that you want to delete.

2. Click Layout menu, In the Rows & Columns tab click the delete option.

3. Choose the Delete Columns option to delete the entire column.

OR, Choose the Delete Rows option to delete the entire row.

Insertion of row or column :

1. Select the cell to insert the row or column above, below, left, or right of the cell.

2. In the Layout menu, In Rows & Columns tab, choose the option to insert the rows or columns.
