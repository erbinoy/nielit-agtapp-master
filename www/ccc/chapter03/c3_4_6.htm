 3.4.6 Alignment of Text..
Changing text alignments:

Text alignment refers to the position of each line of text in a page between the left and right margins. On the Home tab of the Ribbon includes four alignments buttons that can be used to quickly change the alignment of the text.

To change the alignment of a text:

1. Select the text that you want to align.

2. On the Home tab, click the desired alignment button.
