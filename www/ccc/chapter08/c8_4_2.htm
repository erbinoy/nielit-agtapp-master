8.4.2 Adding Shapes

Powerpoint has its own shapes gallery which contains different categories of shares and arts. We can insert the readymadeshapes from this gallery.

Inserting a shape:

1. Select the slide where you want to add or insert the shape.

2. Click Insert menu

3. Click Shapes from the Illustrations tab.

4. Select the shape, point the mouse on the slide where you want to insert, and drag until the size of the shape as you required.
