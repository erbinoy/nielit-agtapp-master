8.4.3 Adding Pictures from file

You can insert pictures in the Powerpoint through Insert menu bar.

Inserting a picture.

1.Click on the slide where you want to insert the Picture.

2.Click Insert menu.

3.Click Pictures present on Images tab.

4.An Insert picture window will appear, open the location of the picture and select.

5.Click Insert.
