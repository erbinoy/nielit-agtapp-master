8.4.4 Resizing and Scaling of a picture

After inserting the picture, it is essential to resize them to fit them in your presentation.

Steps to Resize an image or picture :

1. When you select the picture, sizing handles appear at the corners and along the edges of the selection rectangle.

2. You can resize the picture by dragging its sizing handles or you can resize it more precisely by specifying a percentage for the picture's height and width.

3. If it is a photo or a bitmap or clip art, for example, you can crop it, and you can also restore it later to its original image. For best results when resizing pictures in a presentation that you plan on saving as a Web page, size the picture to a specific percentage under 50.



Steps to Crop an image or picture :

1. Select the picture or image to crop.

2. Click the Format menu from the Standard toolbar.

3. Select Crop from the Size tab.

4. Now you can Crop the picture by dragging its croping handles.

5.Press Enter key.
