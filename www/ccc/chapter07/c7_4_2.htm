7.4.2 Activating Spell Checking

You can do a spell check manually by pressing the F7 key or if you do it the long way, click "Tools > Spelling".

Note: Microsoft Word must be installed for spell checking to work on XP computers!

The most efficient and effective way to check your spelling in outgoing messages that you have composed is to have all your email spell-checked automatically without ever hitting any buttons or making a single mouse click.

There is a way to make spell check come up before an email goes out. Just follow these steps:

1. In Outlook Express, go to Tools > Options then click the "Spelling" tab

2. check the "Always check spelling before sending" box.

3. Click "OK/Apply".

Other email clients, including Web-based email programs, have similar options.

Spell Checker for Outlook Express, adds the ability to use spell checking in Outlook Express without the need to install other MS products such as Word or MS Office
