7.2.3 Configuring Email Client

An email client, email reader or more formally mail user agent (MUA), is a computer program used to manage email . In addition, a web application providing message management, composition and reception functionality is sometimes considered an email client.

Configuring an Email Client is an easy task. On most mail clients just type in your email address and password. Microsoft Outlook Express is a commonly used email client software to compose,send and receive mails.

To configure Outlook Experess, follow the given steps :-

1. Open Outlook Express and click on the Tools menu. Select Accounts.

2. Click on "Add account" and select Mail.

3. Enter your name as you would like it to appear in the "From:" field of all outgoing mail.

4. Enter the email address which the others will use to send email messages to you.

5. Incoming mail server (POP3 server): yourdomain.com

Outgoing mail server (SMTP server): yourdomain.com

Set the SMTP port to 25 or 2525.

6. Username: Use the full e-mail account name. Example: you@yourdomain.com instead of you.

7.Click Finish.

8.Now click once on the newly created account, and choose Properties. Go to the Servers tab and check "My server requires authentication".

If you want the messages to be kept on the server, choose the "Advanced tab" and check "Leave a copy of messages on server", click OK.
