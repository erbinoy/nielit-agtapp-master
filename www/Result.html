Result


    The result will be declared on the NIELIT website http://www.student.nielit.gov.in.
    Under normal circumstances, the result will be declared after 15 days from the completion of examination cycle.
    The examination result is represented as grades. The legend of grades is:
    S: 85% and above,
    A: 75% – 84%,
    B: 65% – 74%,
    C: 55% – 64%,
    D: 50% – 54%,
    E: 40% - 49%,
    F: Fail ( <40% ),
    abs: Absent

Certification


    The CCC certificates to the successful candidates will be issued by the NIELIT HQ.
